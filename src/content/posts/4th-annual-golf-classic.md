## 4th Annual Golf Classic Returns

Show us what you got! Registration for BCRP's 4th Annual Golf Classic is now **OPEN**.

Printed and Online REGISTRATION options are available.

**Date:** Friday, April 10th, 2026

**Location:** ColoVista Golf Club, 100 Country Club Drive, Bastrop, TX

Join us for a day of golf, networking, and fun while supporting the Bastrop County Republican Party. This annual fundraiser brings together Republicans from across the county for a memorable day on one of Central Texas' finest golf courses.

### Registration Options

- **Individual Player:** Register online or download the printed registration form
- **Team Registration:** Form a foursome and save
- **Sponsorship Opportunities:** Multiple levels available to showcase your business

Don't miss out on this premier event! Register today.
