## BCRP's 2026 County Convention

Attention Bastrop County Republican voters. BCRP will hold the Bastrop County Republican Party's Convention.

**Date:** Saturday, March 28, 2026  
**Time:** 10:00 AM  
**Location:** Family Worship Center, 2425 FM 1704, Elgin, TX 78621

The County Convention is a critical part of the Republican Party's process for adopting resolutions, electing delegates, and conducting official party business. All Republicans who participated in the Precinct Convention are eligible to attend.

### What to Expect

- Election of delegates to the State Convention
- Adoption of county resolutions
- Official party business
- Fellowship with fellow Republicans

Mark your calendars and plan to attend this important event in the life of the Bastrop County Republican Party.
