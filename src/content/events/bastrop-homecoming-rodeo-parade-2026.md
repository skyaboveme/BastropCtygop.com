## 🤠 Bastrop Homecoming & Rodeo Parade

Get ready, Bastrop! The Bastrop County Republican Party is thrilled to announce that we will have a float in this year's **Bastrop Homecoming & Rodeo Parade**!

Come out and celebrate our community, heritage, and conservative values with us. We’ll be rolling through downtown Bastrop, and our walking participants will be handing out goodies along the route! Grab the family, wear your red, white, and blue, and look for the big BCRP elephant logo! 🐘🇺🇸

### Schedule & Location

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **When** | Saturday, August 1, 2026 |
| **Time** | Parade starts at 10:00 AM |
| **Where** | Downtown Bastrop (Route begins on Cedar Street heading west to Main Street) |

Let's make this a fantastic homecoming! See you there!
