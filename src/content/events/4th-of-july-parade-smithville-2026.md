## 🇺🇸 4th of July Parade Float – Smithville

The Bastrop County Republican Party is rolling out our float for the **Smithville 4th of July Parade** and **everybody is welcome** to ride or walk!

**We need riders and walkers** — bring your family and friends and show your patriotic spirit!

### Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| **8:00 AM – 9:00 AM** | Registration — RAIN OR SHINE |
| **9:00 AM – 10:00 AM** | Judging |
| **10:30 AM** | Parade Starts |

### Location

**Masonic Lodge**
102 Wilkes St
Smithville, TX 78957
*(Cross streets of Wilkes and 1st)*

### What to Bring

- **Wear patriotic attire** — red, white, and blue!
- Bring water and sunscreen — it's Texas in July!
- A great attitude and your American pride 🇺🇸
