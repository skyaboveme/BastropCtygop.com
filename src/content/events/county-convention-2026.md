## BCRP's 2026 County Convention

Attention Bastrop County Republican voters. BCRP will hold the Bastrop County Republican Party's Convention.

Delegates elected at the Precinct Convention will gather for the County Convention to adopt resolutions and elect delegates to the State Convention.

### Agenda

- Call to Order
- Credentials Report
- Election of Convention Officers
- Adoption of Resolutions
- Election of Delegates and Alternates to State Convention
- Other Official Business
