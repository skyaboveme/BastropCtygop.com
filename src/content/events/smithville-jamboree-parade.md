## Smithville Jamboree Parade

Join the Bastrop County Republican Party at the Smithville Jamboree Parade!

### Schedule

- **10:00 AM** — Meet at the Smithville Recreation Center to decorate the trailer
- **1:15 PM** — Ride on the Parade Float through Smithville

### What to Wear

**Wear Patriotic Attire!** Show your red, white, and blue pride as we ride through the Smithville Jamboree Parade.
