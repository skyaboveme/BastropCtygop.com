## 2026 Precinct Convention

The Precinct Convention is your opportunity to participate in the grassroots Republican process. All registered Republicans who voted in the Republican Primary are eligible to attend their precinct convention.

### What Happens at a Precinct Convention?

- Elect delegates to the County Convention
- Submit resolutions for consideration
- Participate in the Republican Party process from the ground up

### What to Bring

- Your voter registration card or valid photo ID
- Any resolutions you wish to submit
